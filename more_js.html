Ingredients

-2 cups frozen mixed berries
- 1 banana
- ½ cup Greek yogurt
- 1 ¼ cup milk
- 1 ½ tablespoons sugar, maple syrup, or honey
- ½ cup ice
- Optional add-ins: 1 tablespoon almond butter, ¼ teaspoon vanilla, fresh mint leaves

Steps

Place all ingredients in a blender, breaking the banana into pieces. Blend until creamy and frothy, stopping and scraping down the sides as necessary. If desired, garnish with mixed berries and mint sprig. Serve immediately or store in a covered jar in the refrigerator for 2 days.
